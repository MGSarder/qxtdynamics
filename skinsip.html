SKINSIP

A refined, innovative self dispenser blending engineering precision with modern aesthetics in beauty industry.

Overview

Skinsip is a team of six first-year students from Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT) developing an intelligent skincare dispensing system. The device is engineered to deliver a precisely metered dosage of skincare product, targeting users who have limited time or reduced energy to perform traditional skincare routines. Leveraging principles of mechatronic design, fluid control, and user-centric automation, the system ensures both accuracy and convenience. The project was recognized among the Top 10 entries in the Bach Khoa Innovation competition organized by the university’s student board.

Key Features

  • High-precision engineering with modern design
  • Friendly UI/UX design
  • Active reminder and streak system
  • Custom-tailored solutions for client needs
